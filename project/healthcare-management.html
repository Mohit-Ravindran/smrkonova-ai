  • 01.

    Project Overview

    Smrkomed is an all-in-one healthcare management platform that connects doctors, patients, hospitals, clinics, and pharmacies through integrated digital solutions. The platform streamlines healthcare operations with specialized dashboards for each user type, appointment scheduling, and comprehensive medical service management.

    Our solution addresses the complex needs of modern healthcare by providing dedicated interfaces for all stakeholders in the medical ecosystem. From patient appointment booking to hospital administration and medicine management, Smrkomed creates seamless healthcare experiences.

    Core Components Delivered

    Patient Dashboard - Easy appointment booking, medical history, and health tracking
    Doctor Dashboard - Patient management, scheduling, and medical records access
    Hospital/Clinic Admin - Complete facility management and operations oversight
    Medicine Management - Pharmacy integration and prescription handling
    Admin Panel - System-wide administration and analytics
    Appointment System - Smart scheduling across all healthcare providers

    The platform unifies healthcare delivery by connecting all participants through intuitive, role-specific interfaces designed for efficiency and optimal patient care.

    Summery

    Smrkomed successfully delivers a comprehensive healthcare ecosystem that serves patients, doctors, hospitals, clinics, pharmacies, and administrators through one unified platform.
